Images folder
==============

Add your photos here (JPG, PNG, or WebP).

Included placeholders (replace with real photos):
  - placeholder-1.svg … placeholder-4.svg

To use your own files:
  1. Copy photos into this folder (e.g. us-beach.jpg, birthday-2025.jpg).
  2. In index.html, find the gallery section (#gallery).
  3. For each <img> and matching button, set:
       src="images/your-file.jpg"
       data-src="images/your-file.jpg"   (same path, used for the lightbox)
  4. Update data-alt and alt with a short description.

Tip: Square or nearly square photos look best in the grid.
